The platform

One profile. Every proof.

CareerOS connects the places where your work already lives and turns scattered activity into a single, verified professional identity — no manual updates, no copy-pasted portfolios.

How it works

You connect your accounts once. CareerOS reads your public activity, keeps it in sync, and verifies it against the original source — so everything shown on your profile is real and always current.

Behind the scenes it gathers your commits, solved problems, projects and certificates, organises them, and presents them as one coherent story a recruiter can trust at a glance.

What you can connect

Link the platforms you already use. CareerOS handles the rest:

  • GitHub
  • LeetCode
  • GeeksforGeeks
  • LinkedIn
  • Projects
  • Certificates

What you get

Two things, kept up to date automatically: a unified verified profile you can share with a single link, and a recruiter-ready resume generated from the same verified data — so your proof and your paperwork never fall out of sync.
